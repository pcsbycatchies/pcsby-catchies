About Us

Greetings from "PC's by Catchies", our photocard business. We appreciate K-pop fans and even those people who understand the importance of conserving the past in addition to being a part of it. Our customized photocards can assist you in keeping in mind the important events in your life.

PC's by Catchies is founded in November 2023. Driven by our passion for K-pop and preserving our special experiences, we wanted to start a business that would allow others to experience our joy.

Vision

PC's by Catchies aim to provide Korean idol fans with high-quality photocards. Promoting lifelong friendships and fandom among fans throughout the world.

Mission

PC’s by Catchies' mission is to enable people to connect via the art of customised cards. Our commitment to providing exceptional quality and prompt delivery guarantees that each card is delivered.
